Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Marudhar Jewellers respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • Name, contact details, and address for processing orders.
  • Payment details (securely processed by third-party gateways; we do not store card information).
  • Website usage data, such as pages viewed and time spent.
  • Analytics and marketing interaction data (e.g., Meta and Google).

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To improve your browsing experience.
  • To send offers or updates (only if you opt in).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

Your data is shared only with:

  • Delivery and logistics partners.
  • Payment processors.
  • Marketing and analytics tools like Meta and Google.

We never sell or rent customer information.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and tracking pixels (such as Meta Pixel) to improve your experience and marketing relevance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Data Security

We use secure servers and encryption to safeguard your data. While all reasonable precautions are taken, online data transfer carries some inherent risks.

Your Rights

You may request correction or deletion of your personal information by emailing Ashrath@marudharjewellers.com.
